Lil Smart Biography

Dedicated Idowu Smart Emmanuel however turned out to be broadly known as Lil Smart is a 25 years old Nigerian dancer, on-screen character, humorist, model, and lyricist.

Lil Smart hit the spotlight in 2019 after his dance move turned into a web sensation online which increase monstrous reposts from top celebrities in the Nigerian industry.

Not only did Lil Smart gain popularity through originating the “Tesumole” dance move for Naira Marley but his striking resemblance with the controversial Nigerian musician Naira Marley had a major boost in his career.

Early Life

Lil Smart is an indigene of Ogun State Nigeria but was brought up in the Nigerian commercial state, Lagos.

Lil Smart born March 5, 1998, is the originator of reverse Shaku Shaku, Tesumole, reverse Legwork, reverse Kupe, Mafa Mafa, and reverse Soapy.
