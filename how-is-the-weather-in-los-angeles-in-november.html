How Is The Weather In Los Angeles In November

August 2024 · 3 minute read
People are currently reading this guide.

So You Want to Escape the November Blues...And Land in L.A.? Let's Talk Weather!

Ah, November. The month that sits awkwardly between Halloween's sugary rush and December's festive frenzy. The weather, well, it's like a chameleon with a commitment issue – can't quite decide if it wants to be summer's lingering guest or winter's eager apprentice. But fear not, weary traveler with wanderlust for Los Angeles in November! This post is your guide to the glorious (and slightly unpredictable) weather you can expect.

Sunshine State? Not Quite in November, But Still Pretty Darn Good

Let's be honest, California isn't called the Sunshine State for nothing. Even in November, the skies tend to be a cheerful blue, with an average of nine glorious hours of sunlight each day. That's plenty of time to ditch the parka and soak up some rays (don't forget the sunscreen – L.A. sun can still pack a punch!).

But here's the catch: November is also when Los Angeles dips its toes into the shallow end of the rainy season. Don't expect torrential downpours or umbrellas permanently attached to your hand. Think more along the lines of the occasional sprinkle or a quick burst of rain that clears up faster than you can say "Hollywood downpour."

Temperature Tango: From Warm Days to Cool Nights

Think of November's temperature in L.A. as a well-rehearsed tango between warm days and cool nights. Average highs hover around a comfortable 73°F (23°C), perfect for exploring the city in a t-shirt and jeans. But as the day waltzes towards sunset, the temperature dips into the mid-50s (around 10°C). So, pack a light jacket or sweater – you'll thank yourself when that cool evening breeze rolls in.

Pro Tip: Pack layers! This way, you can adjust your outfit depending on whether you're basking in the afternoon sun or strolling along the beach under a blanket of stars.

Don't Forget the Fun Facts (Because Weather Can Be Fun, Right?)

  • Rain, Rain, Go Away (But Not Really): The average rainfall in L.A. during November is a measly 1 inch (25 mm). That's less than a spilled latte!
  • Warmest on Record: Believe it or not, L.A. once saw a sizzling 101°F (38°C) in November. But that's about as likely as running into a real Hollywood A-lister on the street (although, hey, stranger things have happened!)
  • Ocean Dip Anyone?: The Pacific Ocean might not be bathwater-warm, but it's definitely not freezing in November. Average water temperatures hover around 68°F (20°C), which is tolerable for the brave souls who want to take a post-sightseeing plunge.

So, there you have it! November in L.A. is a weather wonderland with a touch of unpredictability. Just pack for sunshine, with a dash of rain showers and cool evenings thrown in for good measure. With a little planning, you'll be ready to embrace the City of Angels and all its glorious (and slightly quirky) November weather.
