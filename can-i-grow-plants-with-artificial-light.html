Can I grow plants with artificial light?

September 2024 · 6 minute read
Plants can indeed grow under artificial light, and they can grow well under the right lighting conditions. While sunlight is great, you don't need it to have a successful indoor garden. If you want to place your plants away from your windows, you don't have to sacrifice growth.

Can plants grow with artificial light only?

Researchers can successfully grow plants using only artificial light in growth chambers. But sunlight is best for most plants. It's generally more intense than artificial light, and it's pretty equally distributed among the different wavelengths that earthly plants have evolved to like best.

What light bulb is closest to natural sunlight?
