Chicago Fire star Charlie Barnett admits being written out of show 'broke' him

September 2024 · 2 minute read

The actor is perhaps best known for starring in Russian Doll

    Chicago Fire star Charlie Barnett has opened up about leaving the hit show, admitting that it "broke" him. The actor, who played Peter Mills on the hit show before leaving in season three, told : "[The show] was a behemoth that I never really saw coming. I was thankful to be a part of it. I really was sad to be let go. It broke me for quite a long time.
