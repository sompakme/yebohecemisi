Kourtney Kardashian's Husband, Travis Barker, Surprises His 2 Kids With G-Wagons Worth N150M Each

  • Kim Kardashian's eldest sister Kourtney and her husband took Christmas gifts to the next level after leaving the drummer's kids in awe
  • Travis Barker surprised his two kids, whom he shares with his ex-wife, with two Mercedes Benz G-Wagons, which cost N150 million each
