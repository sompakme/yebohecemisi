Does QVC have a free shipping code?

If you’re searching for free shipping codes, QVC does have selected products with free shipping. You can find these on its ‘free shipping’ page or by clicking our relevant coupon link. You can also get free shipping with your first auto-delivery shipment.  

When does QVC have sales?

QVC has daily, weekly, and seasonal sales on its website. You can shop its ‘Today’s Special Value & Deals’ or simply type ‘sale’ into the search bar on the QVC website. We’ve previously seen up to 80% off during QVC clearance sales. 

Does QVC have a credit card?

Yes. If you shop regularly at QVC, you might consider a QCard. This is QVC’s credit card which will give you $20 off your first purchase. QVC will also give you VIP savings and early access to sales. 

Can I get QVC gift cards?

Yes, you can get a QVC gift card for your loved one. Simply go to the QVC website and click ‘QVC gift cards’ from the website footer. You can then choose which type of gift card you would like, write out names, and add money to the card.  

What is the returns policy at QVC?

Returns can be made within 30 days at QVC. Your item should be returned in resalable condition with its original packaging. You should include a return form with your return. Your refund will deduct any return shipping costs unless the product is being returned due to defect. 

Hints & Tips

Shop for Today’s Special Value: Check the ‘Today’s Special Value’ promotion on the QVC website to get the best savings for today only. The dedicated team at QVC updates this page daily by discounting one item. Whether you’re searching for pro-whitening toothpaste or a new summer top, this page has high-value items. 

Lunchtime specials: If you want to shop smart, then it’s worth knowing about QVC’s lunchtime specials. These usually run each day from 11 am - 4 pm. You’ll be able to shop for special discounts on selected items for these five hours only. If you miss the lunchtime offers, then you can always shop on the clearance page. 

Newsletter discounts: Sign up for the QVC newsletter to be kept updated on the latest QVC deals. You’ll be kept updated on the latest sales, promotions, and clothing. To sign up, go to the QVC website footer and type your preferred email address into the sign-up box.
