FBOY Island Season 4 will premiere in 2024, at 3 am ET on HBO Max. The series will follow the same format as the previous seasons, with three episodes dropping every week until the finale. Storyline & Plot

FBOY Island Season 4 will feature three new women who will move to a tropical paradise where they will be joined by 24 men, half of whom are self-proclaimed nice guys looking for love, and half of whom are self-proclaimed fboys, there to compete for cold, hard cash. The women will have to navigate the dating pool together, with the help of host Nikki Glaser, and try to find their true matches. By the finale, all will be revealed: who is a nice guy, who is an fboy, who the women ultimately choose, and who walks away with the prize money.
