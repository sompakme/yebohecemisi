Squid Game villain Jang Deok-su's home life has shocked everyone - photos

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Heo Sung-tae has shared all on Instagram

    Heo Sung-tae takes on the role of gangster and ringleader Jang Deok-su in Netflix's hit series , and the show focuses on him being a bully, but you may be surprised to learn that his home life is oh-so different. Revealing his softer side, the actor clearly has a heart of gold unlike his on-set persona and in real-life he is a pet parent to two adorable cats.
