Olivia Rodrigo’s Upcoming GUTS Tour

Olivia Rodrigo GUTS Tour is just around the corner, and you might be wondering how to snag those precious presale tickets. We’ve got you covered with all the details you need.

What Is a Presale Code?

Before we dive in, let’s talk about what a presale code is. It’s like a secret password that lets you buy tickets before they’re available to the general public. Olivia’s tour has two presale options, so let’s break them down.

 Official Platinum Presale

On September 20, 2023, at 3 pm local time, the Official Platinum Presale kicks off. To join in, you’ll need something called “The Registration Access Code.” Keep in mind that these tickets might be a bit pricier than the regular ones. But if you’re a die-hard Olivia Rodrigo fan, it might just be worth it.

 American Express Presale

There’s also an American Express Presale, but the exact start date is still a mystery. The cool thing about this one is that you don’t need a special code. If you sign up and get selected, you can use your AMEX card to buy tickets early for the North American tour dates. No code needed, just your trusty AMEX card.

How to Buy Presale Tickets

Now that you know the types of presales available, let’s talk about how to buy those tickets. It’s a straightforward process.

 Log In to Ticketmaster

First, you’ll need to visit Ticketmaster’s website. If you don’t have an account, go ahead and create one. Then, log in.

 Choose Your Concert Date

Pick the date you want to see Olivia in concert. Make sure it works with your schedule.

Sign Up for a Presale Code

Now, here’s the important part. Sign up for a presale code. You’ll receive an email on either September 19 or September 20 for American Express Early Access. This email will let you know if you’re good to go or if you’re on a waiting list.

Ticket Prices

Now, let’s talk money. Regular tickets for Olivia’s tour usually range from $49.50 to $199.50. But remember, these prices might not include taxes and additional fees. So, budget accordingly.

Olivia Rodrigo’s Rise to Stardom

Before we wrap it up, let’s take a quick look at Olivia Rodrigo’s journey to stardom. She’s not just any artist; she’s a talented American singer-songwriter and actress. Olivia got her start in Disney TV shows, where she wowed audiences with her acting chops. She’s even bagged three Grammy Awards and has a whopping net worth of $16 million.

 Conclusion

So there you have it, folks! The scoop on how to score those coveted Olivia Rodrigo GUTS Tour presale tickets.
