Who is Devyn Nekoda? Wiki, Age, Bio, Net Worth, Career, Relationship,Family

September 2024 · 2 minute read
  • Devyn Nekoda, born on December 12, 2000, in Brantford, Canada, is a young and talented Canadian Actress, Dancer, and Social Media Personality.
  • She gained popularity for her role as Vanessa Morita in the show “Backstage” and has a passion for dancing, which she began at the tender age of 2, training under Lisa Naves Dance Company.

A Journey to Stardom

  • Devyn Nekoda’s journey to stardom led her to enroll in the Toronto School of Circus and Acts, where she landed the lead role in the “An American Girl” series alongside Polish Ballroom Dancer, Edyta Sliwinska.
  • Apart from “Backstage,” Devyn has showcased her talent in other shows such as “The Next Generation,” “The Next Step,” and “Spotlight.”
  • Her on-screen performances have garnered positive reviews, and she has amassed a fan following of 114.5k on Instagram.

Stepping into the World of “Northern Rescue”

  • Devyn recently appeared in the recurring role of Allison in “Northern Rescue,” a Netflix show comprising 10 episodes that started on March 1, 2019.
  • The series revolves around John, who moves to a small town with his three children after the death of their mother. The family struggles to adjust to their new life, and the show features talented actors like William Baldwin, Spencer MacPherson, Taylor Thorne, and Amalia Williamson.
