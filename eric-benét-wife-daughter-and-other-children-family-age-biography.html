Eric Benet Bio (Age)

Eric Benét Jordan was born on November 15, 1966 in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. He is the youngest of five children. When Eric was younger, he formed a band called Benét with his older sister Lisa and George Nash Jr – his cousin. The band released their debut album in 1992 and sold over 100,000 copies.

Just two years later, Eric re-emerged on the music scene as a solo artist and signed a record deal with Warner Bros. Records. In 1996 he released True to Myself, his first solo album with Warner Bros. Records. In 1999 he released A Day in the Life, his second album. This album featured “Spend My Life With You (ft. Chipmunk)”, one of his greatest singles. The song reached number one on the US Billboard R&B charts. It was also certified gold and nominated for “Best R&B Performance by a Duo or Group” at the 2000 Grammy Award show. The album also won several accolades, including the Soul Train Music Award for “Best R&B/Soul Album, Male”.
