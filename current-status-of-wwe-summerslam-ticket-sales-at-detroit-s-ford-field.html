Current Status of WWE SummerSlam Ticket Sales at Detroits Ford Field

August 2024 · 2 minute read

WWE’s SummerSlam event is going down on August 5th at Ford Field in Detroit. Fans area already hyped about the big show, and plenty of tickets have already left the box office at this point. Naturally, they’re looking at a loaded show, one that is likely to be a total sellout.

WWE receives a ton of interest to host their big 4 events. You can check out spoiler on one city who is bidding on a future Royal Rumble event here.

Wrestle Tix noted behind their paywall that WWE tickets sales are going very strong for SummerSlam. At this point, with four months to go, WWE is well on their way to selling every seat out that they possibly can at Ford Field.

WWE SummerSlam [Ford Field, Detroit, MI] update (new sections added)
WWE SummerSlam
Sat • Aug 05 • 7:30 PM
Ford Field, Detroit, MI

Available Tickets => 4,381
Current Setup => 43,689
Tickets Distributed => 39,308

It was also noted that, “Some new upper deck sections added (back 4 rows unopened at the top levels).” Obviously, things are doing very well for WWE in terms of SummerSlam ticket sales.

WWE always brings a lot of attention to the host cities for every one of their big events. SummerSlam’s 2023 event will be a massive show in Ford Field, and only time will tell what matches WWE books for the biggest party of the summer.
