What Kate Chastain Has Been Up To Since Leaving Below Deck

July 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Kate Chastain has taken on new television gigs since finishing her longtime position on "Below Deck." Starting in 2021, Chastain joined "Below Deck Galley Talk," in which fan-favorite former "Below Deck" cast members share their thoughts on the latest episodes of the show, per The A.V. Club. More recently, Chastain has begun to appear as a contestant on "The Traitors." The Peacock program centers around 20 contestants who are competing for a sizable cash prize. As the hopefuls vie for the money in the Scottish Highlands, three contestants have been designated as "the traitors" and attempt to find a way to steal the money from their competitors. The show is hosted by Alan Cumming, and features other reality stars such as Brandi Glanville and Ryan Lochte.

Meanwhile, Chastain has revealed major news in her personal life. In December 2022, she announced via an Instagram post that she is pregnant with her first child. "I'm already planning so many birthday theme parties for you," Chastain wrote while showing off her baby bump. Other TV personalities responded to Chastain with congratulatory comments. "Eeeeek the news is out!! Sooooo excited for you my angel," Fraser Olender of "Below Deck" wrote. "Southern Charm" star Madison LeCroy added, "How did I miss this!?? Congratulations." Additionally, Chastain has publicly stated how she feels about a possible return to "Below Deck."
