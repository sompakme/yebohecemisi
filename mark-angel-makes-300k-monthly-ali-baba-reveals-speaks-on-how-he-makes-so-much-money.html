Mark Angel Makes $300K Monthly: Ali Baba Reveals, Speaks on How He Makes So Much Money

August 2024 · 4 minute read
  • Veteran Nigerian comic Ali Baba recently made a stunning revelation about how wealthy comedians are
  • He revealed while on Nedu Wazobia's Honest Bunch that Mark Angel is one of the richest comedians in Nigeria, if not the wealthiest
  • Ali Baba shared with Nedu how Mark Angel earns $300k monthly from Facebook and YouTube, and he doesn't look it at all
  • Legit.ng in response to Ali Baba's recent comments while he was on Nedu's show, reacted to him for a chat about the growth of comedy in Nigeria

The godfather of Nigeria's comedy, veteran comic Atunyota Alleluya Akpobome, better known as Ali Baba, was recently on a show where he made a stirring revelation about the young comedian Mark Angel.

During an interview with Ndeu Wazobia's Honest Bunch, Ali Baba revealed that Mark Angel is one of Nigeria's wealthiest comedians.

"Stand-up comedy is going nowhere," Ali Baba says

After his recent comments on Nedu's show about Mark Angel, Legit.ng reached out to Ali Baba. During our conversation, we asked him if, very soon, skit makers would chase Stand-up comedians away, considering that there seems to be more money in skit making. He said:

"Stand-up is going nowhere. Two truths can co-exist. However, I would love to note that instead of skit makers chasing stand-up, they're rather delving into it because that's where the real money is. Sabinus now has his own stand-up, Woli Agba does, Coach Remote and many others. Like I said, stand-up is going nowhere."
