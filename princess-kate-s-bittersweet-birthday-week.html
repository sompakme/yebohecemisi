The Princess of Wales celebrates her 41st birthday on 9 January

    The Princess of Wales is set to celebrate her 41st birthday on Monday 9 January, but the royals will also be bracing themselves for revelations in Prince Harry's memoir, Spare.

    Kate spent the festive period with her young family in Norfolk, joining King Charles and Queen Consort Camilla at Sandringham for Christmas.
