UPDATE: Actor Femi Adebayo and wife names baby boy in the United States (photos)

July 2024 · 3 minute read

Legit.ng earlier reported that popular Nigerian actor Femi Adebayo Salami and his beautiful wife, Omotayo Maimunat Sanusi welcomed their first baby in December 2018.

The bouncing baby was safely delivered in the United States of America and the proud father took to his Instagram handle to share the good news.

However, ever since little Adebayo has been born, the couple have kept the picture of the baby to themselves until Wednesday, January 2, when Omotayo decided to share a rare picture of him on her Instagram handle.

Omotayo shared a loved-up picture of her and Femi in blue traditional outfits as she carried her baby who might be sleeping or smiling at his mother. She only revealed a side picture of the baby as she is not ready to show off his face.

UPDATE:

The beautiful picture was taken at the naming ceremony of Femi and Omotayo's baby boy in the US. The Islamic ceremony took place at Oga Bello's house as he also shared pictures from the ceremony on his Instagram handle.

Oga Bello shared a picture of Femi and his wife with the caption: "We thank God for the success of Fadhil 's naming ceremony here and abroad. We thank everyone who supported at all levels. May Allah continue to bless your respective households. Compliments of the season to you all."
