University of Manitoba - Winnipeg, Manitoba

York University - Toronto, Ontario

Training costs in the central part of Toronto are very high. However, if you decided to study in Toronto, then York University, which is located in the northern part of the city, will be a very profitable option for you. In contrast to the central areas of Toronto, the cost of education is much lower here, and regarding education, York University is not inferior to central universities at all.

The lack of urban attractions of this university perfectly compensates for its capacity, excellent study programs, and lower tuition fees, unlike universities in the center of Toronto.

This university is an excellent choice for students who want to get postgraduate studies in Canada. The University of Waterloo is in a couple of hours of drive from Toronto and offers outstanding options for doctoral programs. Simon Fraser University - Burnaby, British Columbia

Simon Fraser University has recently become one of the most highly rated universities among international students. The university offers:

  • a wide range of academic programs
  • affordable tuition fee
  • the choice among three campuses located in Burnaby, Surrey, and Vancouver

University of Alberta - Edmonton, Alberta

The university is located in the northern part of Canada, and therefore the weather conditions here are quite harsh. However, all this can be immediately forgotten as soon as it comes to the cost of training. If you are looking for cheap schools in Canada for international students, then the University of Alberta is one of the best choices.

McGill University - Montreal, Quebec

The main advantages of studying at this university for international students are the low cost of education and the well-developed infrastructure of Montreal. The city develops according to European trends and has an excellent atmosphere for learning and recreation.

The university will be especially relevant for those international students who know French. A large number of theaters, art studios, and music institutions, contribute to the annual growth of the number of international students at McGill University.

However, it should be understood that the cost of studying and living in Toronto is one of the most expensive in Canada.

University of British Columbia - Vancouver, British Columbia

The University of British Columbia offers almost the most expensive tuition fees. However, the high cost is adequately compensated by:

  • the most beautiful panoramas
  • a variety of campuses from small to large
  • access to one of the best research grants in the world
  • a wide range of training programs

This university should be considered closely if you plan to stay in Canada after graduation. It is Vancouver that leaves the most vivid and pleasant impressions of life in Canada among international students.

The availability of international programs and a varied range of tuition fees, as well as a diverse cultural environment, are good factors for studying in Canada for international students.
