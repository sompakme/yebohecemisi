Who Is Annie Idibia?

Annie Idibia, also known as Annie Macaulay-Idibia, is a Nigerian actress, model, and presenter. She was born on November 13, 1984 in Ibadan, Nigeria.

She is well known for her performances in the movies Beautiful Monster and Morning After Dark. In 2016, she received the Fast Rising Actress award at the African Entertainment Legend Awards.
