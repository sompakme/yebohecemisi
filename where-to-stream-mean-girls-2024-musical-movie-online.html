Where to Stream 'Mean Girls' (2024) Musical Movie Online

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Twenty years after the infamous Burn Book, movie lovers can now sing along to Mean Girls at home.

The musical twist on Tina Fey’s 2004 cult-favorite teen comedy is now available to stream on Paramount+. It’s also available to buy or rent on digital at Prime Video, Apple TV and other on-demand video platforms. The film belted past box office expectations in January with its $33.2 million opening.

Original Mean Girls Broadway star Reneé Rapp reprises her onstage role on the big screen as queen bee of “the Plastics” Regina George (first played by Rachel McAdams). The musical reboot also stars Angourie Rice as Cady Heron (originally portrayed by Lindsay Lohan); Auli’i Cravalho and Jaquel Spivey as her friends, as Janis Ian and Damian Leigh; and Jenna Fischer as Cady’s mother. Avantika and Bebe Wood round out the Plastics as Karen Shetty and Gretchen Weiners (first played by Amanda Seyfried and Lacey Chabert), while Busy Philipps is Regina’s mom.

Screenwriter Fey returns as Ms. Norbury, along with Tim Meadows, who is once again Principal Duvall. Ashley Park (who starred in the Broadway musical), John Hamm, Mahi Alam and Connor Ratliff also star in the new movie.

Below, keep reading to find out where else to watch Mean Girls — including the hit 2004 film — online.

Where to Stream Mean Girls (2024) Online

Mean Girls (2024) is now streaming online at Paramount+. The film is also available to buy or rent in 4K Ultra HD digital at transactional video-on-demand platforms, including Prime Video and Apple TV.

New subscribers of Paramount+ can watch Mean Girls for free with a seven-day trial to the streamer. After that, the ad-supported Paramount+ Essential plan is $5.99 per month (or $59.99 per year) while the Paramount+ with Showtime tier is $11.99 monthly (or $119.99 annually) and only has ads on live TV and select series.

Only Paramount+ subscribers get access to Mean Girls alongside live access to CBS News, NFL on CBS and UEFA Champions League; next-day streaming for CBS series, including Ghosts, NCIS, Survivor and more; Paramount+ Originals shows such as Halo, Emmy-nominated Lawmen: Bass Reeves, 1883 and Frasier; and films like Mission: Impossible — Dead Reckoning Part I, Transformers: Rise of the Beasts, Top Gun: Maverick; Scream VI, Good Burger 2 and many others.

Paramount+ with Showtime subscribers also enjoy live TV (including CBS and college football), Showtime original series and movies (like Emmy-nominated shows such as The Curse and Yellowjackets) and the ability to download shows to mobile devices to watch later.

Where to Stream Mean Girls (2004) Online

The original Mean Girls (2004), starring Lindsay Lohan and Rachel McAdams, is available to stream on demand at Paramount+.

You can also buy or rent Mean Girls digitally on Apple TV, Prime Video and other video-on-demand services. The made-for-TV 2011 sequel, Mean Girls 2 is also available on Prime Video, Apple TV and elsewhere.
