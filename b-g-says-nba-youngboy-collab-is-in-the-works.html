B.G. Says NBA YoungBoy Collab Is In The Works

August 2024 · 3 minute read

In September of last year, B.G. was finally released from federal prison after serving more than a decade for gun and witness tampering charges. The New Orleans-born performer has wasted no time getting back into things, hitting the studio only days after he got out. Since his release, he's worked alongside the likes of Boosie Badazz, Sexyy Red and Kevin Gates, Gucci Mane, and more. He plans to continue making the most of his newfound freedom, recently teasing yet another high-profile collab.

Earlier this week, B.G. was chatting with fans on Instagram Live when one user asked him if he'd consider collaborating with NBA YoungBoy. According to him, the project is already underway. In a new clip, the Hot Boys rapper assures commenters that his project with YB will arrive soon. “I hollered at YB yesterday, man," he revealed. "Ya heard me? It’s coming. It’s coming. That’s a fact."

B.G. Teases NBA YoungBoy Collab On IG Live

Clearly, B.G. isn't looking to slow down in 2024, and fans can't wait to see what else he has up his sleeve. As for YB, Judge Shelly D. Dick recently loosened the 24-year-old's house arrest restrictions following his team's motion citing his declining mental health. He's now allowed to leave "Gravedigger Mountain" for medical appointments, court appearances, and other activities approved by his pretrial supervision officer.

He also recently spoke with Gillie and Wallo on their Million Dollaz Worth Of Game podcast, sharing his plans to pull back on some of his vices. "[I had to go to rehab] two weekends ago, I think," he explained. "Now I'm kind of taking on just therapy from now on. I've been doing a lot of rich sipping, and smoking these nasty a** cigarettes.
