Cast, Plot &amp; Everything You Need to Know

September 2024 · 4 minute read

Never Have I Ever Season 4 Release Date in India Time, Cast, and Plot:- Never Have I Ever is one of the most popular and acclaimed comedy dramas on Netflix. The show, created by Mindy Kaling and Lang Fisher, follows the life of Devi Vishwakumar (Maitreyi Ramakrishnan), a first-generation Indian American teenager who deals with the loss of her father, the pressures of high school, and the complexities of love.

The show has won over fans and critics alike with its witty humor, diverse representation, and heartfelt storytelling. The first three seasons have been binge-worthy hits, and fans are eagerly waiting for the fourth and final season to see how Devi’s story ends.

Here is everything you need to know about Never Have I Ever season 4: the release date in India time, plot, cast, story, and updates.

Never Have I Ever Season 4 Release Date in India Time

Never Have I Ever season 4 will premiere on Netflix on June 8, 2023. The show will be available to stream at 1:30 PM IST (Indian Standard Time) on the same day. All 10 episodes will be released at once, so you can watch them at your own pace.

Plot

Never Have I Ever season 4 will follow Devi’s senior year at Sherman Oaks High School. She will have to balance her academic goals, her family issues, and her romantic dilemmas.

In season 3, we saw Devi break up with Paxton (Darren Barnet), her dreamy jock boyfriend, after realizing that they had different plans for the future. She also hooked up with Ben (Jaren Lewison), her nerdy rival-turned-friend, but decided to keep it casual. However, things got complicated when a new student named Ethan (Michael Cimino) joined the school and caught Devi’s eye.

Season 4 will explore how Devi navigates her feelings for these three guys, while also applying to her dream college Princeton. She will also have to deal with her mother Nalini (Poorna Jagannathan), who is dating a new man named Chris (Common), and her cousin Kamala (Richa Moorjani), who is pursuing a career in biotech.

Cast

The cast of Never Have I Ever season 4 will feature the return of all the main characters from the previous seasons. Here are some of them:

  • Maitreyi Ramakrishnan as Devi Vishwakumar
  • Poorna Jagannathan as Nalini Vishwakumar
  • Richa Moorjani as Kamala Nandiwadal
  • Jaren Lewison as Ben Gross
  • Darren Barnet as Paxton Hall-Yoshida
  • Michael Cimino as Ethan
  • Ramona Young as Eleanor Wong
  • Lee Rodriguez as Fabiola Torres
  • Sendhil Ramamurthy as Mohan Vishwakumar (in flashbacks)
  • Common as Chris Jackson
  • Niecy Nash as Dr. Jamie Ryan (Devi’s therapist)

The show will also feature some guest stars and cameo appearances from celebrities like John McEnroe (as himself and the narrator), Andy Samberg (as himself), Chrissy Teigen (as herself), Mindy Kaling (as herself), and more.

Story

The story of Never Have I Ever season 4 will wrap up Devi’s journey as a teenager who struggles with grief, identity, culture, and love. The show will also address some important themes like mental health, racism, sexism, sexuality, friendship, family, and more.

The show will also pay tribute to its creator Mindy Kaling, who announced that she is ending the show after four seasons. She said that she wanted to give Devi a proper send-off and leave the fans satisfied.

“I’m so grateful to Netflix and Universal Television for letting me tell this story about a young Indian American girl who just wants to live her life,” Kaling said in a statement. “It’s been an honor to work with such a talented cast and crew who brought so much joy and laughter to this show. I’m also thankful to the fans who have supported us from the beginning. I hope you’ll love this final season as much as we do.”

Updates

Never Have I Ever season 4 has been in production since January 2023. The show has been filming in Los Angeles, California, under strict COVID-19 protocols. The cast and crew have been sharing some behind-the-scenes photos and videos on social media, giving fans a glimpse of what to expect.

Never Have I Ever season 4 is set to be a hilarious, emotional, and satisfying conclusion to one of the best shows on Netflix. Don’t miss it when it drops on June 8, 2023, at 1:30 PM IST.
