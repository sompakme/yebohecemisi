Ronaldo's $350k donation to earthquake victims in Syria and Turkey

August 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Football superstarCristiano Ronaldo has made a generous donation to the victims of the recent devastating earthquake in Syria and Turkey.

The former Manchester United player was moved by the tragic scenes and has sent a plane loaded with care items to help those affected.

The Portugal legend has paid for the transportation of tents, food packages, pillows and blankets, beds, baby food and milk, and medical supplies to aid the humanitarian effort in the affected areas of Turkey and Syria. This donation is valued at $350,000.

In the past, he has shown his generosity by paying $83,000 to fund a child's brain surgery and donating $165,000 towards a cancer center in his native Portugal. Ronaldo, who has served as an ambassador for Save the Children, UNICEF, and World Vision, has also donated $1 million to Portuguese hospitals during the height of the coronavirus pandemic.

Ronaldo's latest act of kindness has been applauded by fans and followers across the globe. His compassion and generosity towards the less fortunate have set an excellent example for other celebrities and individuals. The football star's contributions are a ray of hope for those impacted by the earthquake and show that even small acts of kindness can make a significant difference in people's lives.

Cristiano Ronaldo has been named the Roshn Saudi League Player of the Month for February. This is his first individual award since joining the Saudi League side.

Ronaldo expressed his excitement on Twitter, saying that he was "happy to win the February Player of the month award for the Roshn Saudi League. Hopefully the 1st of many! Proud to be part of this team". He also added that this award is "the first of many" and that he was "proud" to win for Al-Nassr.

In addition to winning the Player of the Month award, Ronaldo also celebrated scoring his 500th career goal in his side's 0-4 win at Al Wehda in February, where he netted all four goals.

Ronaldo's success in the Saudi Arabian league has cemented his status as one of the greatest footballers of all time, and his fans around the world are looking forward to seeing what he will achieve next.
