Who is Jack Donnelly? Wiki, Age, Bio, Net Worth, Career, Relationship,Family

July 2024 · 3 minute read

Jack Donnelly is a talented British actor, model, theater artist, and businessman known for his association with the London-based comedy group “Chuckle Duster” and being the husband of the Canadian-Swedish actress and model, Malin Åkerman. Let’s explore more about this versatile individual:

Movies, Career & Filmography

  • Jack made his acting debut in 2011 on Nickelodeon’s series “House of Anubis,” playing the role of “Jason Winkler” in 31 episodes.
  • He has also appeared in guest roles in various TV shows like “Threesome,” “Misfits,” “Dancing on the Edge,” and “Doctors.”
  • In 2013, he got his big break as the lead role of “Jason” in the British fantasy TV series “Atlantis,” earning praise for his performance.
  • His first movie was “United We Fall” (2014), and he impressed fans with his role as “Prince Adrian” in the film “A Royal Winter” (2017).
  • Currently, Jack is working on the upcoming comedy movie “Friendsgiving,” set to release in 2019, where he will star as “Jeff,” alongside his wife Malin Åkerman as “Molly.”
