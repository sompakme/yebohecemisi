Is Layla a good main?

September 2024 · 4 minute read

Layla is a strong pull in Genshin Impact if players do not have shielders or additional Cryo support. Despite having underwhelming damage, she can still become a valuable asset for many teams with enough investment.

Is Layla worth building?

Layla may be a sleepy Akademiya student, but she is a very productive member of your team if you build her right. Layla’s Elemental Skill, Nights of Formal Focus, deals Cryo damage in an area around her while creating a shield, the strength of which is based on her maximum HP.

What is Layla’s best artifact set?
