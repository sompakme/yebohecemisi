Muslim girl, 17, sparks outrage after twerking in public while wearing her hijab (photos)

September 2024 · 2 minute read

A Muslim girl has caused serious outrage on social media after she was caught twerking in public while wearing her hijab, as people cheered her on.

The Sun UK reported that the 17-year-old was out shopping with pals when she and a friend joined a street performer and began dancing.

Footage of her dancing was later uploaded online, and it attracted a barrage of hateful comments. One wrote: “That’s so disrespectful is you are wearing hijab you are representing Islam respect dignity so how to act like a fool that is a big disrespect.”
