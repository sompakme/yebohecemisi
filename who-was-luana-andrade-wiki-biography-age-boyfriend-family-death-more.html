Who was Luana Andrade? Wiki, Biography, Age, Boyfriend, Family, Death &amp; More

September 2024 · 6 minute read

Luana Andrade (born 21 January 1994 – died 7 November 2023, Aged: 29 years old) was a Brazilian model, social media influencer, and businesswoman who had a large following on Instagram and other platforms.

She was known for her beauty, style, and healthy lifestyle. She died tragically on November 7, 2023, due to complications during a liposuction procedure. Here is everything you need to know about her life, career, and death.

Biography

Luana Andrade Lopes was born on January 21, 1994, in São Paulo, Brazil. She was the only child of Luciana Andrade and Mr. Andrade. She grew up in a middle-class family and had a happy childhood. She attended a local school and later pursued a degree in advertising and propaganda.

She also underwent some cosmetic surgeries to enhance her appearance, such as breast augmentation and rhinoplasty. However, her decision to do a liposuction procedure proved fatal for her, as she suffered a cardiac arrest during the surgery and died. She was 29 years old when she passed away.

What Happened to Luana Andrade?

According to reports, Andrade had the surgery performed by a private doctor and anesthesiologist who were hired by her family at the Hospital São Luiz. The procedure was supposed to last about an hour, but about two and a half hours into the surgery, the influencer experienced a “respiratory event” and went into cardiac arrest.

The surgery was interrupted and the patient underwent tests that revealed massive thrombosis, which is a condition where blood clots block veins or arteries. She was transferred to the intensive care unit (ICU) where she received medication and hemodynamic treatment. However, she did not respond to the interventions and was pronounced dead around 5:30 a.m. on Tuesday.

The official cause of death was pulmonary embolism, which is a sudden blockage of an artery in the lung, usually caused by a blood clot that travels from the leg. This is a rare but serious complication of liposuction, especially when the surgery involves large areas of the body or multiple procedures.

Death and Cause

Luana Andrade died on November 7, 2023, at a private hospital in São Paulo, Brazil. She was 29 years old. She had undergone a procedure to remove body fat from her abdomen and legs at the hospital. However, she suffered a cardiac arrest just before the surgery and was rushed to the intensive care unit. She did not respond to the treatment and passed away after a few hours.
